# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how **Mandala Grid** (hereinafter referred to as "this Application") collects, uses, and protects your information.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy is intended to explain how we handle information collected through this Application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### Personal Data
This Application **will not** require you to create an account, nor will it actively collect your personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, or phone number).

### Local Data Storage
All content you create in the Application is stored in a local database on your device (using Room Persistence Library). We cannot access, upload, or backup this content to our servers. If you delete the Application, these local data will be lost unless you have performed a system-level backup.

### Third-Party Services
In order to provide advertising services, this Application uses third-party services. These services may collect information used to identify you.

Links to privacy policies of third-party service providers used by this Application:
* [Google Play Services](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/)
* [AdMob](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543?hl=en)

## 2. Log Data
When an error occurs while using this Application, we may collect data and information on your device through third-party products (referred to as "Log Data"). This Log Data may include your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the Application when utilizing this Application, the time and date of your use of the service, and other statistical data.

## 3. Cookies
Cookies are files containing a small amount of data that are typically used as anonymous unique identifiers. This Application does not explicitly use these "Cookies". However, the Application may use third-party code and libraries that use "Cookies" to collect information and improve their services.

## 4. Service Providers
We may employ third-party companies and individuals for the following reasons:
* To facilitate our service;
* To provide the service on our behalf;
* To perform service-related services; or
* To assist us in analyzing how our service is used.

These third parties have access to your personal information for the purpose of performing these tasks on our behalf. However, they are obligated not to disclose or use the information for any other purpose.

## 5. Security
We value the personal information you have provided to us, and thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 6. Children's Privacy
This service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete such information from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take the necessary actions.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Therefore, we recommend that you check this page periodically for changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at any time.
